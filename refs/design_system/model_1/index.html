06 · FAQ

Questions teams ask before getting started.

Everything you need to know about setting up Pulsedesk, inviting your team, and keeping projects organized.

View FAQ Contact us

How quickly can my team start using Pulsedesk?

Most teams create their first workspace, add a project, and invite collaborators in just a few minutes. The workflow is intentionally simple, so there is no long setup process.

Is Pulsedesk built for individuals or teams?

Both. Freelancers can use it to manage personal client work, while teams can coordinate tasks, owners, deadlines, comments, and project progress in one shared place.

Can we customize projects and workflows?

Yes. You can organize work with projects, task statuses, owners, priorities, due dates, and notes without turning the workspace into a complicated system.

Can Pulsedesk replace spreadsheets and scattered notes?

For task tracking and project coordination, yes. Pulsedesk keeps responsibilities, timelines, updates, and progress visible so teams do not need to chase information across tabs.

Is our workspace secure?

Pulsedesk is designed around secure workspaces and clear access for your team. You control who is invited and what projects they collaborate on.

What happens if we need more capacity later?

You can start small and expand when your team needs more projects, reporting, collaboration controls, or support. Pulsedesk is built to scale without adding clutter.
